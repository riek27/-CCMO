What We Do

Integrated programs, lasting change.

Our work spans eight interconnected program areas — each designed to reinforce the others.

Our Approach

Programs that reinforce each other.

CCMO's integrated model means that peacebuilding, education, WASH, protection, livelihoods, and infrastructure are not separate silos — they are interlocking pieces of a holistic strategy for community resilience. A borehole serves a school; a peace dialogue creates the conditions for learning; a livelihood program gives families the stability to keep children in class.

Peacebuilding, Protection & Human Rights

Conflict Mitigation · GBV Response · Human Rights Advocacy

CCMO's peacebuilding program is at the core of our mission. We facilitate inter-communal dialogues, train peace committees, and mediate conflicts over resources such as water, grazing land, and cattle. Our protection work includes specialized GBV prevention and response — with safe spaces, psychosocial support, and referral pathways for survivors. We also advocate for the rights of people with disabilities, ensuring their inclusion in all community decision-making.

45+Peace Dialogues
30+Peace Committees
65%Reduction in Raiding

Education & Capacity Building

Primary Education · Adult Literacy · Teacher Training · Peace Education

Our flagship education program delivers quality learning opportunities to children and adults across South Sudan. We build and rehabilitate classrooms, train teachers, provide learning materials, and integrate peace education into every curriculum. Our adult literacy program empowers community members — especially women — with the foundational skills they need to participate fully in civic and economic life.

10+Schools Supported
20+Teachers Trained
500+Students Reached

WASH — Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

Borehole Drilling · Latrine Construction · Hygiene Promotion

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right — and a critical foundation for peace. CCMO drills boreholes, rehabilitates water points, constructs latrines, and delivers hygiene education in schools and communities. Our WASH interventions reduce waterborne disease, improve school attendance (especially for girls), and create shared resources that bring communities together.

25+Washing Programs
500+People with Clean Water
20+Latrines Built

Food Security & Livelihoods

Agriculture · Cash-for-Work · Income Generation · Emergency Food Aid

Food insecurity and economic vulnerability fuel conflict. CCMO's livelihood programs address both immediate needs and long-term resilience. We provide emergency food assistance during crises, train farmers in climate-resilient agriculture, distribute seeds and tools, and run cash-for-work programs that inject economic value into communities while building essential infrastructure.

500+People Assisted
500+Farmers Trained
5Cash-for-Work Projects

Youth & Women Leadership

Leadership Training · Mentorship · Governance Education

Sustainable peace requires the full participation of youth and women in decision-making. CCMO delivers leadership training, mentorship programs, and governance education that equips young people and women to take active roles in community leadership, peace processes, and local governance structures.

50+Youth Leaders Trained
80+Women in Leadership
10Mentorship Cohorts

Health & Emergency Response

Emergency Relief · Health Services · Shelter · NFI Distribution

During crises — whether conflict, floods, or displacement — CCMO provides rapid, multi-sectoral emergency assistance. Our response includes health outreach, shelter support, and distribution of non-food items such as blankets, cooking utensils, and hygiene kits to displaced and vulnerable populations.

Infrastructure Development

School Construction · Road Rehabilitation · Water Infrastructure

Physical infrastructure is the backbone of community resilience. CCMO builds and rehabilitates schools, health posts, water systems, and access roads — employing local labor through cash-for-work to ensure skills transfer, economic benefit, and community ownership of every project.
