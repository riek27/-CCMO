Support Our Work

Every act of solidarity matters.

Your support — whether financial, in-kind, or through partnership — directly funds classrooms, boreholes, peace dialogues, and youth leadership programs on the ground.

Why Your Support Matters

Every contribution creates ripples of change.

In South Sudan, communities face overlapping crises — conflict, displacement, food insecurity, and lack of access to education and clean water. Your support enables CCMO to respond with integrated, community-led solutions that address immediate needs while building long-term resilience.
