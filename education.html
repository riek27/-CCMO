Education for peace.

Jonglei State · Education Hub
Why Education Matters

Education is the foundation of lasting peace.

In communities fractured by conflict, education does more than teach — it restores hope, builds social cohesion, and equips the next generation with the tools to break cycles of violence. Children in school are less likely to be recruited into armed groups. Literate adults participate more fully in peace processes and local governance. And when girls are educated, entire communities benefit — with lower rates of child marriage, improved health outcomes, and stronger economies.

Our Goals

What we're working toward.

Our education program has clear, measurable goals that guide every project.

01

Access to Education

Increase enrollment and reduce barriers — especially for girls, children with disabilities, and those in remote areas.

02

Quality Learning

Train teachers, provide learning materials, and create safe, well-equipped learning environments.

03

Girls' Education

Break down cultural and economic barriers that keep girls out of school — through scholarships, awareness, and safe facilities.

04

Adult Literacy

Empower adults — especially women — with reading, writing, and numeracy skills for civic and economic participation.

Activities

What education looks like on the ground.

From building classrooms to training teachers, here's how we deliver quality education.

School Construction & Rehabilitation

Building and renovating classrooms, latrines, and playgrounds to create safe, dignified learning spaces.

Teacher Training

Professional development for 200+ educators — covering pedagogy, classroom management, and peace education.

School Supplies

Distributing textbooks, notebooks, pencils, and teaching aids to under-resourced schools.

Peace Education Curriculum

Integrating conflict resolution, tolerance, and human rights into every subject at every grade level.

Vocational Training

Equipping youth and adults with marketable skills — from carpentry and tailoring to computer literacy.

Scholarships

Providing financial support to the most vulnerable students, ensuring that poverty does not block the path to learning.
