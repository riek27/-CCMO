New Community School Opens in Jonglei — 300 Children Enrolled

CCMO's latest education facility brings quality learning to children in Fangak County, complete with trained teachers, learning materials, and a school feeding program. The school features 8 classrooms, separate latrines for girls and boys, and a dedicated peace education curriculum.

WASH
June 18, 2026

12 New Boreholes Bring Clean Water to 8,000 People

CCMO's WASH teams have completed drilling across three counties in Lakes and Unity states. The new boreholes provide safe drinking water to approximately 8,000 people, dramatically reducing waterborne illness and freeing women and girls from hours of daily water collection.

May 28, 2026

Youth Leadership Program Graduates 50 New Peace Advocates

Fifty young leaders from across South Sudan have completed CCMO's intensive governance and peacebuilding training in Juba. The graduates — 28 women and 22 men — return to their communities equipped to lead peace initiatives and mentor other youth.

CCMO Hosts Regional Peace Conference in Juba

Over 200 participants — including community leaders, government officials, and NGO partners — gathered in Juba for a two-day conference on grassroots peacebuilding strategies. Key outcomes included a commitment to expand peace committees across all 7 states.
