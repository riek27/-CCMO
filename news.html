Education
MARCH 15, 2026

CCMO Launches Education Program in Bentieu — 300 Children Enrolled

The first phase of our flagship education program is now underway, delivering quality primary education, teacher training, and peace education to children in Fangak County. The new school facility will serve as a model for expansion to other states.

WASH
MAY 10, 2026

WASH Program Gains Momentum — Clean Water and Sanitation Reach More Communities

CCMO's WASH program is actively delivering safe drinking water, constructing latrines, and conducting hygiene promotion campaigns across multiple states. Field teams are working directly with communities to reduce waterborne diseases and improve daily life.

Partnerships
JULY 5, 2026

CCMO Partners with UNDP to Implement Livelihoods and Resilience Program in Unity State

Through this new collaboration, CCMO will deliver climate‑smart agriculture training and cash‑for‑work activities, targeting 500 households. The partnership will strengthen food security and economic recovery in conflict‑affected areas.

Sanitation
JUNE 22, 2026

Community-Led Toilet Building Initiative Reaches 200 Households

CCMO's sanitation drive is tackling open defecation head‑on, constructing 200 household latrines across Eastern Equatoria. The initiative promotes dignity, improves public health, and empowers communities to sustain their own sanitation facilities.

Education
APRIL 18, 2026

120 Teachers Complete Professional Development Training

CCMO's latest teacher training cohort — 120 educators from 25 schools — completed an intensive program covering inclusive pedagogy, peace education, and classroom management. Participating schools report improved student engagement and learning outcomes.

Education
FEBRUARY 2026

Accelerated Learning Brings Hope to Out‑of‑School Children

CCMO's education program has reached 500 children in Lakes State who never attended school or dropped out. Using accelerated learning methods, children aged 8–14 are now catching up on core subjects and preparing for formal schooling.
