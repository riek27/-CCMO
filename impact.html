Program Results

Results by program area.

Peacebuilding

  • 45+ peace dialogues facilitated
  • 30+ peace committees established
  • 65% reduction in cattle raiding (Unity State)
  • GBV safe spaces in 15 communities

Education

  • 8,000+ students enrolled
  • 200+ teachers trained
  • 45+ schools supported
  • 40% increase in girls' enrollment

WASH

  • 25+ boreholes drilled
  • 12,000+ people with clean water
  • 50+ latrines constructed
  • Hygiene promotion in all target schools

Livelihoods

  • 5,000+ people received food assistance
  • 500+ farmers trained
  • 15 cash-for-work projects completed
  • Income generation for 300+ households
Timeline

Our journey since 2022.

2022

Founded in Juba

Registered as a national NGO (REG NO 5759). Began operations with peace dialogues in Central Equatoria.

2023

Expanded to 4 States

Launched education and WASH programs. Reached 50+ communities. Trained 100+ teachers.

2024

Integrated Program Model

Added livelihoods, GBV protection, and infrastructure programs. Peace dialogues expanded to Unity State.

2025

7 States · 100+ Communities

Major growth in reach and impact. Landmark peace agreement mediated in Unity State. 200+ teachers trained.

2026

Scaling for Peace

15,000+ lives touched. New community school opened in Jonglei. Expanding to reach more communities.
