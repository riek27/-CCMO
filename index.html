Who We Are

From conflict to community-led resilience.

Founded in 2022, CCMO was born from a simple but urgent truth: while national peace agreements exist, local conflicts driven by resource competition, cattle raiding, and revenge killings continue to fracture communities. We exist to bridge that gap — with peacebuilding, education, and access to clean water at the heart of our mission.

Our integrated approach combines peacebuilding, education, WASH, humanitarian assistance, and sustainable development — because lasting peace requires dignity, knowledge, and the most fundamental resources.

Education

CCMO Launches Education Program in Bentieu — 300 Children Enrolled

The first phase of our flagship education program is now underway, delivering quality primary education, teacher training, and peace education to children in Fangak County. The new school facility will serve as a model for expansion to other states.

WASH
May 10, 2026

WASH Program Gains Momentum — Clean Water and Sanitation Reach More Communities

CCMO's WASH program is actively delivering safe drinking water, constructing latrines, and conducting hygiene promotion campaigns across multiple states. Field teams are working directly with communities to reduce waterborne diseases and improve daily life.

CCMO Partners with UNDP to Implement Livelihoods and Resilience Program in Unity State

Through this new collaboration, CCMO will deliver climate‑smart agriculture training and cash‑for‑work activities, targeting 5,00 households. The partnership will strengthen food security and economic recovery in conflict‑affected areas.
